Zilinghan Li

I am an MSCS student University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, and research assistant at Argonne National Lab. I also interned at AWS. I recently worked on research topics around Federated Learning, Distributed Computing, and Large Language Models, with students, researchers, and scientists from UIUC, NCSA, and ANL. I am the technical lead for developing the Argonne Privacy-Preserving Federated Learning framework.
