Investigation of MapReduce Algorithms on Streams using the BSP Bridging Model

  • Advisor: Klaus-Dieter Schewe, ZJU-UIUC Institute
  • Classifying stream queries into three tpyes according to their concatenation properties: memoryless, semi-memoryless and memorable.
  • Implementing memoryless and semi-memoryless stream queries using MapReduce Algorithm in the spirit of BSP parallel computaion model.
  • For memorable stream queries, the limit case of semi-memoryless stream queries, is more of theoretical interests. To deal with such case in theory, much more agents are required in the parallel compuation, so we extend the expressiveness of BSP model and develop a new behavioral theory of BSP algorithm with infinite number of available agents.
